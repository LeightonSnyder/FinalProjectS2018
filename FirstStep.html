The first step to fixing a problem is admitting you have one.

So does the U.S. have a gun violence problem?

Let's look at the numbers...

U.S. citizens own 48% of all the civilian owned guns around the world.

The U.S. has the most guns per capita in the world, 40% higher than the next highest country, Yemen.

The U.S. makes up less than 5% of the world's population, but holds 31% of the worlds mass shooters.

So yes, compared to the rest of the developed world, we have a disproportionate amount of guns and gun violence, but...
